<p><p>The Portable Ambulatory Ventilators market comprises three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. </p><p>Electronic ventilators utilize microprocessor-controlled systems, offering precise ventilation settings and advanced monitoring features, making them suitable for critical care. Pneumatic ventilators rely on gas pressure to function, providing simplicity and reliability but with fewer advanced features. Electro-pneumatic models combine both electronic controls and pneumatic power, delivering enhanced performance and versatility for varied patient needs, bridging the gap between traditional and modern ventilation solutions.</p></p>
