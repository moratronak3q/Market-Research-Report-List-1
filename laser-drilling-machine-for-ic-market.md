<p><p>Laser drilling machines for integrated circuit (IC) manufacturing utilize various types of laser technologies, including YAG (Yttrium Aluminum Garnet) lasers, CO2 lasers, and other laser types. YAG lasers are known for their precision and ability to drill through hard materials, making them suitable for micro-hole drilling in ICs. CO2 lasers offer efficient cutting and drilling capabilities in non-metallic materials but may have limitations on thickness. Other types include fiber lasers, which provide enhanced speed and precision for versatile applications in IC production.</p></p>
