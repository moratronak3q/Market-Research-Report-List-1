<p><strong>The Radiation Processing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotope Radiation Processing</li><li>Accelerator Radiation Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation processing market consists mainly of two types: isotope radiation processing and accelerator radiation processing. Isotope radiation processing utilizes radioactive isotopes, such as cobalt-60 or cesium-137, to sterilize medical instruments and enhance food safety through irradiation. In contrast, accelerator radiation processing employs electron beams or X-rays generated by particle accelerators, providing precise control over radiation levels, making it suitable for applications like materials modification and high-throughput sterilization. Both methods are crucial for improving product safety and quality across various industries.</p></p>
