<p><p>The medical membranes market includes various materials such as Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), and Polypropylene (PP). PTFE is known for its high chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it ideal for applications like dialysis and filtration. PVDF offers excellent thermal stability and is often used in drug delivery systems. Polypropylene is favored for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, commonly utilized in sterile packaging and filtration processes. Together, these materials enable advancements in medical devices and healthcare solutions.</p></p>
