<p><ul><li>IP65</li><li>IP67</li><li>IP68</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterproof luminaire market is categorized based on the Ingress Protection (IP) ratings, which indicate the level of protection against dust and water. IP65 luminaires are dust-tight and protected against water jets, making them suitable for indoor and outdoor applications. IP67 offers complete protection against dust and can withstand temporary immersion in water, ideal for harsher environments. IP68 provides the highest protection, allowing continuous immersion in water, suitable for applications like underwater lighting and in demanding industrial settings.</p></p>
