<p><p>The Nickel Powder for Plating market comprises primarily two types: carbonyl nickel powder and electrolytic nickel powder. Carbonyl nickel powder is produced through the thermal decomposition of nickel carbonyl gas and is known for its fine particle size and high purity, making it ideal for electroplating applications. On the other hand, electrolytic nickel powder is produced through an electrolytic process, offering good fluidity and surface characteristics, suitable for various plating techniques. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the performance and durability of plated surfaces.</p></p>
