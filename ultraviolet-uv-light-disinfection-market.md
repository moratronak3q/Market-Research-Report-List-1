<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Light Disinfection Market includes several key types. Ordinary cathode low pressure mercury UV disinfection utilizes low-pressure mercury lamps to emit UV light, effectively inactivating microorganisms. High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection employs high-intensity lamps for faster and more efficient disinfection across larger areas. Ozone UV disinfection combines UV radiation with ozone generation, enhancing microbial inactivation by producing reactive ozone. Each type offers distinct advantages in efficiency, application, and effectiveness for various disinfection needs across industries.</p></p>
