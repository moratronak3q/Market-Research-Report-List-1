<p><p>The epoxy repair mortar market is primarily divided into two types: two-component and three-component epoxy mortars. Two-component epoxy mortars consist of a resin and a hardener, offering excellent adhesion, durability, and chemical resistance for various surface repairs. Three-component epoxy mortars include an additional component, typically a filler, enhancing properties like thickness and texture. Both types are used in construction, maintenance, and industrial applications, providing strong solutions for repairing concrete and other substrates, ensuring longevity and resilience against harsh conditions.</p></p>
