<p><p>The artificial wood panel market comprises several types, including Particleboard (PB), made from wood chips and adhesives, offering affordability for furniture and cabinetry. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) utilizes finer wood fibers for a smooth surface, ideal for detailed designs. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) provides enhanced durability and moisture resistance. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) consists of strands of wood, providing structural strength. Plywood layers wood veneers for stability and versatility. Other variants may include niche products for specialized applications, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
