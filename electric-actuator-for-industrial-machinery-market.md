The market is primarily divided into two types: DC electric actuators and AC electric actuators. DC electric actuators are favored for their precise control, quick response times, and portability, making them ideal for applications requiring variations in speed and position. In contrast, AC electric actuators are known for their robustness, efficiency, and ability to handle higher power loads, making them suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications.</p></p>
