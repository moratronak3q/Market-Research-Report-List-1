<p><p>The pressure-reducing (boost) valve market primarily includes two types: pressure-reducing valves and boost valves. Pressure-reducing valves are designed to lower and maintain downstream pressure within a specified range, ensuring system safety and efficiency. Boost valves, on the other hand, increase pressure in systems where it is insufficient, enhancing fluid flow and performance. Both types are crucial in various applications, including water supply, HVAC, and industrial processes, ensuring optimal pressure management in pneumatic and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
