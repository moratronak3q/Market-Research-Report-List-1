<p><p>Children's life insurance can be categorized into two main types: term child life insurance and permanent child life insurance. </p><p>Term child life insurance provides coverage for a specified period, typically until the child reaches adulthood, offering lower premiums but no cash value accumulation. Both types aim to provide financial security for families in case of unforeseen events.</p></p>
