<p><p>The monoclonal mouse antibody market is categorized into several types based on the antibody class: IgM, IgG, IgA, and others. IgM antibodies are the first responders in immune response, often used in early detection assays. IgG, the most abundant antibody, is crucial for targeting pathogens and is widely used in diagnostics and therapeutics. IgA plays a role in mucosal immunity, particularly in respiratory and gastrointestinal health. The "Others" category includes less common classes and engineered variants, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
