The Traction Control System (TCS) prevents wheel spin during acceleration by adjusting engine power and braking. Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) maintains a set speed while automatically adjusting to traffic conditions, improving fuel efficiency. The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lockup during hard braking, enhancing vehicle control. Together, these systems help reduce accidents, optimize performance, and increase overall safety in commercial transportation.</p></p>
