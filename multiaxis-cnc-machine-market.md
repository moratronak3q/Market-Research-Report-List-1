<p><p>The multiaxis CNC machine market consists of various types, each serving different manufacturing needs. A 2-axis CNC machine typically handles basic movements along two perpendicular axes, ideal for simple tasks. The 3-axis machine adds vertical movement for more complex parts. The 4-axis variant introduces rotation around one axis, enhancing machining capabilities. The 5-axis machine offers the highest precision by allowing simultaneous movement along all five axes, making it suitable for intricate designs and high-end applications.</p></p>
