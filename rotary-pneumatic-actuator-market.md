<p><strong>The Rotary Pneumatic Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Acting Pneumatic Actuator</li><li>Single Acting Pneumatic Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary pneumatic actuator market is primarily divided into two types: double acting and single acting pneumatic actuators. Double acting pneumatic actuators use compressed air to move the actuator in both clockwise and counterclockwise directions, providing greater control and positioning accuracy. In contrast, single acting pneumatic actuators utilize air pressure to move in one direction while relying on a spring mechanism for return movement, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types are widely used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
