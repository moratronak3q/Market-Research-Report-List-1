<p><strong>The Automotive Composite Leaf Springs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transversal</li><li>Longitudinal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive composite leaf springs market is divided into two main types: transversal and longitudinal springs. Transversal leaf springs are mounted across the vehicle, providing stability and strength, often used in light-duty applications. In contrast, longitudinal leaf springs are aligned along the length of the vehicle, enhancing load-carrying capacity and ride quality, typically favored in heavier vehicles. Both types utilize advanced composite materials, offering benefits like reduced weight, improved durability, and enhanced performance compared to traditional steel springs.</p></p>
