<p><p>PBT yarn, derived from polybutylene terephthalate, is categorized primarily into three market types: POY (Partially Oriented Yarn), FDY (Fully Drawn Yarn), and other specialty yarns. POY serves as a precursor for texturing and further processing, offering versatility in applications. FDY is fully drawn, providing high strength and stability, suitable for direct use in fabrics. The "Other" category includes niche products tailored for specific industrial or consumer applications, enhancing the overall versatility of PBT yarn in the textile market.</p></p>
