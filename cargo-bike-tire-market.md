<p><strong>The Cargo Bike Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Tire</li><li>Tubeless Tire</li><li>Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cargo bike tire market features three main types: tube tires, tubeless tires, and solid tires. Tube tires, which have an inner tube, are common for their ease of repair but can lead to punctures. Tubeless tires eliminate the inner tube, offering better puncture resistance and lower rolling resistance. Solid tires, made from rubber or foam, are devoid of air, providing maximum durability and zero puncture risks, making them ideal for heavy loads, although they might compromise ride comfort.</p></p>
