<p><p>The fully automatic carton sealer market is segmented into two main types: single drive belt sealers and conveyor belt sealers on both sides. Single drive belt sealers utilize one drive belt for transporting cartons, providing efficient sealing with minimal complexity. In contrast, conveyor belt sealers on both sides employ dual belts to enhance stability and speed during the sealing process, accommodating larger volumes and heavier cartons. Both types are essential for improving packaging efficiency in various industries, ensuring consistency and reliability.</p></p>
