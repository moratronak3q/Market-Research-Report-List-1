<p><ul><li>Electron Beam Inspection System</li><li>Bright-Field Inspection System</li><li>Dark-Field Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer defect inspection system market comprises several technologies, including Electron Beam Inspection (EBI), Bright-Field Inspection, and Dark-Field Inspection. EBI utilizes focused electrons to detect defects with high sensitivity and resolution, ideal for advanced nodes. Bright-Field Inspection employs transmitted light to identify surface defects, effective for larger defects. In contrast, Dark-Field Inspection uses scattered light to reveal sub-surface and smaller defects, enhancing detection capabilities. Together, these systems play a critical role in ensuring the quality and reliability of semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
