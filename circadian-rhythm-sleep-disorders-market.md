<p><p>Circadian rhythm sleep disorders encompass various conditions that disrupt the body's natural sleep-wake cycles. Delayed Sleep Phase Disorder (DSP) leads to late sleep and wake times, while Jet Lag Disorder results from travel across time zones. Irregular Sleep-Wake Rhythm features fragmented sleep patterns, and Free-Running Type occurs without external cues, causing inconsistency. Shift Work Disorder affects those with non-standard work hours, and Advanced Sleep Phase Disorder (ASP) involves early sleep and wake times. Each type poses unique challenges for management and treatment.</p></p>
