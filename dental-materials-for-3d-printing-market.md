<p><p>The dental materials for 3D printing market is categorized into three main types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent materials are used for clear aligners and mock-ups, offering aesthetics and visibility. Translucent materials mimic the natural tooth appearance, making them ideal for crowns and bridges. Opaque materials, typically employed for functional dental components like dentures, provide strength and durability without light transmission. Each type serves specific applications, catering to the diverse needs of dental professionals in restorative and orthodontic treatments.</p></p>
