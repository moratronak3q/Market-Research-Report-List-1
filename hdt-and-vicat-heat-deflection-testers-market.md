<p><p>HDT (Heat Deflection Temperature) and VICAT (Vicat Softening Point) testers are crucial instruments used to evaluate the thermal properties of materials, particularly polymers and plastics. These tests are vital for ensuring that materials can withstand elevated temperatures during processing and application.</p><p>The HDT and VICAT Heat Deflection Testers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.6% during the forecast period. Emerging trends include the integration of automated testing solutions and the use of advanced materials that require precise thermal analysis.
