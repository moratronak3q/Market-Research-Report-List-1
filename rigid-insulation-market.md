<p><p>The rigid insulation market primarily consists of Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), and other materials. EPS is lightweight, cost-effective, and offers good thermal insulation properties, making it suitable for various applications. XPS, while denser and more moisture-resistant, provides superior thermal performance and is often used in demanding environments.
