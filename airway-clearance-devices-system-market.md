<p><p>The Airway Clearance Devices System market includes various types designed to enhance mucus clearance in patients with respiratory conditions. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices create pressure during exhalation, helping to keep airways open and mobilize secretions. Flutter devices utilize a ceramic ball to create vibrations, aiding mucus discharge. Other devices encompass chest wall oscillation systems and handheld nebulizers, providing alternative methods for effective airway clearance. Together, these devices improve respiratory function and overall patient health.</p></p>
