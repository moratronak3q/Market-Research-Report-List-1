<p><p>Cancer tumor profiling market comprises various technologies used to analyze and characterize tumors. Next-generation sequencing (NGS) enables comprehensive genomic analysis for identifying mutations. Polymerase chain reaction (PCR) is utilized for amplifying specific DNA sequences, facilitating targeted mutations detection. Immunohistochemistry (IHC) assesses protein expression in tissue samples, providing insights into tumor characteristics. In Situ Hybridization (ISH) visualizes specific nucleic acid sequences within cells, while microarray technology allows simultaneous examination of multiple genes. Other methods encompass diverse profiling techniques, enhancing personalized cancer treatment.</p></p>
