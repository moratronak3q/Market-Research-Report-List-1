<p><p>The medical disposable protective clothing market is primarily categorized into two types: polyethylene and polypropylene. In contrast, polypropylene clothing is breathable and lightweight, offering comfort and protection from dust and non-hazardous particles. Both types cater to different healthcare settings, with polyethylene being favored for its fluid resistance and polypropylene for its comfort during prolonged use.</p></p>
