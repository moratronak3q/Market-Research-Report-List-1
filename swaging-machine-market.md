<p><ul><li>Radial Swaging Machine</li><li>Rotary Swaging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The swaging machine market is categorized mainly into two types: radial swaging machines and rotary swaging machines. Radial swaging machines use a series of dies to compress and shape metal by applying radial force, ideal for producing precise, uniform parts. In contrast, rotary swaging machines employ a rotating mechanism that gradually shapes the material, allowing for continuous processes and efficient production of complex geometries. Both types cater to various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and metalworking.</p></p>
