<p><ul><li>External elimination</li><li>Internal elimination</li><li>Half Interna elimination</li><li>Industrial emission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submersible effluent pumps are categorized based on their design and application in wastewater management. </p><p>- **External elimination** pumps discharge fluids outside the pump housing, typically used for transferring effluent over long distances. </p><p>- **Internal elimination** pumps discharge within a closed system, offering efficiency in compact spaces. </p><p>- **Half internal elimination** combines features of both, providing flexibility in installation. </p><p>- **Industrial emission market** focuses on robust pumps designed for heavy-duty applications, addressing high volume and challenging effluent types in industrial settings.</p></p>
