<p><p>The amniotic membrane market is primarily divided into two types: cryopreserved amniotic membrane and lyophilized amniotic membrane. Cryopreserved amniotic membrane is preserved at low temperatures to maintain its biological properties, providing a ready-to-use option for surgical applications. In contrast, lyophilized amniotic membrane undergoes a freeze-drying process, allowing for extended shelf life and easier storage. Both types are utilized in various medical fields, including ophthalmology and wound healing, owing to their unique biological and healing properties.</p></p>
