<p><strong>The Neuroprosthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Output Neuroprosthetics</li><li>Input Neuroprosthetics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neuroprosthetics can be categorized into output and input neuroprosthetics based on their functions. Output neuroprosthetics are devices that assist or restore motor functions by stimulating muscles or nerves, enabling movement in individuals with paralysis or limb loss. Examples include prosthetic limbs controlled by neural signals. Conversely, input neuroprosthetics focus on sensory restoration, such as devices that provide auditory or visual feedback, helping users regain lost sensations. Together, these technologies enhance the quality of life for individuals with neurological disabilities.</p></p>
