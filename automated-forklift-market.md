<p><p>The automated forklift market is categorized into various navigation types, including vision navigation, Lidar navigation, and others. Vision navigation utilizes advanced camera systems and artificial intelligence to interpret the environment, allowing forklifts to navigate complex spaces. Lidar navigation relies on laser-based sensors to create precise 3D maps of surroundings for accurate movement. Other navigation methods may include magnetic strips or inertial guidance systems. Each technology contributes to enhancing efficiency, safety, and operational flexibility in warehouse and material handling applications.</p></p>
