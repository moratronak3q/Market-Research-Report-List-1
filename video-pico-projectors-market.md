<p><strong>The Video Pico Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video pico projectors market primarily consists of two types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors to reflect light and are known for their brightness and color accuracy, making them popular for portable applications. In contrast, LCoS projectors use liquid crystals aligned on silicon chips, offering superior resolution and better contrast, ideal for high-quality image reproduction. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in various settings, from personal to professional use.</p></p>
