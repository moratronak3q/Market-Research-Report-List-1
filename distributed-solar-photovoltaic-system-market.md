<p><p>The distributed solar photovoltaic (PV) system market consists of two primary types: rooftop solar PV systems and ground-mounted solar PV systems. Rooftop systems are installed on residential or commercial buildings, utilizing existing structures to generate electricity, enhancing energy independence and reducing utility bills. In contrast, ground-mounted systems are installed on open land, often in utility-scale projects, allowing for larger installations that can harness significant solar energy output. Both types contribute to decentralized energy generation and sustainability goals.</p></p>
