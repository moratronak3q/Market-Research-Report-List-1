<p><ul><li>Electro-optic Modulator</li><li>Acousto-optic Modulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulse picking system market includes two main types: electro-optic modulators (EOM) and acousto-optic modulators (AOM). EOMs utilize electric fields to modulate light, offering high speed and precision, making them ideal for applications in telecommunications and laser systems. AOMs employ sound waves to control light, providing flexibility in frequency and intensity modulation, commonly used in laser science and imaging technologies. Both types enhance the control of light pulses, enabling advancements in various optical applications.</p></p>
