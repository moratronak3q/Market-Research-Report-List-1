<p><p>The crop seed treatment market is categorized into three primary types: liquid, granular, and powder. Liquid treatments are typically applied using seed coating techniques, ensuring even coverage and penetration, which aids in disease and pest protection. Granular treatments are easy to apply and provide a slow-release action, enhancing the sustainability of the treatment. Powder treatments are advantageous for their ease of use and effectiveness in adhering to seed surfaces, improving germination rates and seedling vigor while offering essential protection.</p></p>
