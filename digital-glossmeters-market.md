<p><p>The digital glossmeters market is categorized into three main types: Three-Angle Glossmeters, Dual-Angle Glossmeters, and Single-Angle Glossmeters. Three-Angle Glossmeters measure gloss at multiple angles, providing comprehensive surface analyses suitable for various finishes. Dual-Angle Glossmeters offer measurements at two angles, balancing detail and practicality for moderate gloss evaluation. Single-Angle Glossmeters, designed for specific applications, measure gloss at one defined angle, making them ideal for consistent, straightforward assessments of surface sheen across different industries.</p></p>
